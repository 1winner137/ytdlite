Privacy Policy

At yt-dlite, user privacy is a top priority. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or usage data. All downloads and activities remain entirely local to your device, ensuring complete user confidentiality and control. No analytics, no hidden trackers — just pure functionality. Your trust matters to us, and yt-dlite is committed to safeguarding it by design.
